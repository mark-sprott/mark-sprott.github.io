What is a responsive website and why is it important?

A responsive website or page is able to adapt, depending on which sort of device you are viewing it in. This means that weather you are using a laptop, tablet or mobile-phone the page you are looking at will look the same. If the website or page was not responsive then you would find that when you viewed it on a mobile-phone then parts would be missing.
