What did I think about meditation prior to reading chapter 2 of Searh inside yourself?

I found this chapter very interesting. I have been doing meditation on a regular basis for a couple of months using the 'Headspace app' which I have downloaded onto my phone. I find that doing regular meditation has been very beneficial for me, in both my work and personal life. It is something that I look forward to each morning, and a great way to start the day.

What new things have I learnt about meditation?

Over the last couple of months I have learnt so much about meditation.

My ability to stay focused on things has improved greatly. I have found this very beneficial to my study, but also when having conversations with people, the ability to stay focused and remain present has been very beneficial and exciting.

Meditation techniques that stood out for me

The technique that stands out the most for me is the ability to stay relaxed and alert at the same time. This is the key to good meditation. I would also say that meditation like most things gets better and easier with practice.

Any thing else?

I would engourage everyone to give meditation a try. It can seem difficult at first, but stick with it as the benefits are amazing and can be life changing.
